About Us

Manarat Almadina Engineering

Manarat Almadina Engineering (منارات المدينة للهندسة) is an engineering consultancy headquartered in Madinah, Saudi Arabia, specializing in thermomechanical engineering and sustainability solutions.

Our mission is to deliver technically rigorous, efficient, and sustainable engineering solutions for the Kingdom's built environment — partnering with clients from early feasibility through commissioning.

Our vision is to be a trusted engineering partner for thermal and sustainability challenges across Saudi Arabia — technically credible locally, and built to international standards.

Why We're Based in Madinah

Madinah's climate, growth, and development pipeline demand rigorous thermal engineering and sustainability-first thinking. Being based here keeps us close to our clients and to the realities of building performance in the Saudi context — while our practice is built to serve projects across the Kingdom, in alignment with Saudi Vision 2030.
